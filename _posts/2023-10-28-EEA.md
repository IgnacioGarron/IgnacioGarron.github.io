My paper "Monitoreando los riesgos de la inflación en Bolivia a partir de la distribución de las expectativas de inflación" won 1st place in the call for papers. The BCB awarded a central prize of Bs 35,000 to the best research paper.

- Inflation risk indicator will be available soon in this website.
