Uribe. Prior to this, I worked as a Senior Financial Analyst at the <a href="https://www.bcb.gob.bo">Central Bank of Bolivia (BCB)</a> and as an Economist at the <a href="https://www.cemla.org/index.html">Center for Latin American Monetary Studies (CEMLA)</a>.
